Why I Got Into Coaching

Coaching gives me a break from my everyday stress. During my Junior and Senior years of high school, I felt a lot of pressure to perform well and get recruited. It left me feeling unmotivated and overwhelmed. Coaching provided me with peace and a renewed sense of purpose. Connecting with the kids brings me joy, and teaching basic fundamentals has even helped me improve in my own sports.
